Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, testing procedures, and more.

You can easily book a test through our website by visiting the Booking page, selecting your desired test package, choosing a convenient date and time, and filling in your contact information. You will receive a confirmation email with all the details.

Most test results are available within 24 hours. Some specialized tests may take 2-3 days. You will receive your results via email and they will also be available in your online patient portal.

Yes, we offer convenient home sample collection services for most tests. Simply select the home collection option when booking your test, and our trained phlebotomist will visit your home at your preferred time.

Yes, MEDILAB is NABL certified and follows strict quality control protocols. All our tests are conducted using state-of-the-art equipment and our team consists of highly qualified professionals.

Preparation varies by test type. For fasting blood tests, avoid eating for 8-12 hours before the test. For other tests, specific instructions will be provided when you book. You can also call us for detailed preparation guidelines.

Yes, all your test results are stored securely in your online patient portal. You can access and download them anytime. If you need physical copies, please visit our center with a valid ID.

We accept most major insurance plans. Please contact our billing department or check with your insurance provider to confirm coverage for specific tests.

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, online payments, and insurance. Payment can be made at the time of sample collection or through our online portal.

We maintain a 99.9% accuracy rate through rigorous quality control measures, regular equipment calibration, and participation in external quality assessment programs. Our results are reviewed by experienced pathologists.

Yes, we offer consultation services with our expert pathologists and physicians. You can schedule a consultation appointment through our website or by calling our office.
